### What is Token in blockchain
A token in blockchain is **a digital assset** presenting **value, ownership, access rights or utility** within decentralized applications (dApps) and are managed by smart contracts, **distinguishing them from native coins**

#### Main Types of Tokens:
- Utility Tokens: Provide access to a specifuc product or service within a platform
- Governance Tokens: Grant holders voting and decision rights within a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO)
- Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs): Represent unique, non-interchangeable digital items 
- Security Tokens: Represent ownership of real-world assets like stocks or real estate
