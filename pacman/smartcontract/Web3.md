### What is Web3?

Web3 refers to decentralized apps that run on the blockchain

#### Pros (Advantage)
- Anyone who is on the network has permission to use the service--or in other words, permission isn't required
- No one can block you or deny you access to the service
- Payments are built in via the native token, ether
- Web3 servers can't go down - they use Ethereum, a decentralized network of 1000s of computers as their backend

#### Cons (Disadvantages)
- Scalability: Changes to state, like a payment, need to be processed by a node and propages throughout the network
- The lack of integration in modern web browsers makes web3 less accesible to most users
- Cost -- most dapps put very small portions of their code on the blockchain as it's expensive
