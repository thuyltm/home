### What is Ethereum?
Ethereum is a blockchain. 
The Ethereum Virtual Machine is a software engine that **acts as the runtime environment** for smart contracts on the Ethereum blockchain. It processes transactions accross thousands of nodes
- Every Ethereum node keeps a copy of the state of this computer
- Whenever a computational task is broadcast, the network, rather than a central authority, verify, validate, and execute the computation. This executaion causes a state change in the EVM, which is propagate throughout the entire network

### What is ether?
Ether is the native cryptocurrency of Ethereum. Any participant who broadcasts a transaction request must also offer some amount of ETH to the network as a bounty. The network will burn **part of the bounty and award who eventually does the work** of verifying the transaction, executing it, commiting it to the blockchain, and broadcasting it to the network
